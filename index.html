About Me

My Story

I'm a first-generation college student — something I carry with pride. My journey has taken me from Government Post Graduate College for Women in Vehari, to one of Pakistan's most prestigious universities, to a graduate program in the United States.

At Indiana University Bloomington, I'm pushing the boundaries of data science — exploring questions at the intersection of sustainability, computing, and our planet's future. My work sits at the crossroads of real-world impact and rigorous research.

I believe data is more than numbers — it's a lens for understanding the world and a tool for changing it. Whether it's reducing carbon footprints or designing smarter data infrastructure, I'm motivated by work that matters.

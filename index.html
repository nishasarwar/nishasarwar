About Me

My Story

I am a first-generation student — something I carry with pride and am learning to embrace more openly. My journey has taken me from Government Post Graduate College for Women in Vehari, to one of Pakistan's most prestigious universities, to a graduate program in the United States. I am a proud Fulbright Scholar.

At Indiana University Bloomington, I research sustainable computing and climate data. Outside academia, I work with companies as a data engineer — helping them structure, understand, and actually use their data. They bring the problem and the domain knowledge. I bring the technical infrastructure to make it actionable.

I believe whether it's reducing carbon footprints or designing smarter data infrastructure, data is more than numbers — it's a lens for understanding the world and a tool for changing it.
